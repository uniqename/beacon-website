Organization Details
Readiness Checklist
ItemYes / NoIf No — Action Needed & Date
A. Legal & Governance
Organization is legally registered (or registration is in active process)
A named Executive Director or equivalent is in post
A functioning board or advisory committee exists (min. 3 members)
A bank account with dual-signatory controls is open
Basic financial record-keeping is in place
B. Staff & Capacity
At least 1 full-time staff member dedicated to survivor support
Staff lead has experience in social work, counseling, or survivor services
Staff are willing to complete Beacon trauma-informed care training
A code of conduct and safeguarding policy exists for staff and volunteers
C. Physical Shelter
A physical location has been identified or secured
The location is in a reasonably discreet area (not easily traced by perpetrators)
The shelter has lockable entry and secure sleeping areas
Running water, sanitation, and cooking facilities are available
Minimum capacity of 3 beds exists (5–8 target)
D. Community & Partnerships
A documented community need exists (data, assessments, or referral demand)
At least 1 legal aid organization has been identified as a potential partner
At least 1 healthcare or counseling provider has been identified
The organization has existing relationships with the target community
A plan exists to address cultural barriers specific to this context
E. Funding & Sustainability
Minimum 6 months of operating budget is secured or committed
A fundraising plan or grant pipeline exists for Year 1
The organization has experience managing donor or grant funds responsibly
F. Data & Survivor Safety
A confidentiality and data protection policy exists (or will be adopted from toolkit)
Secure storage (physical or digital) exists for survivor files
Leadership understands that survivor identities are never shared externally
An incident reporting process exists or will be created
G. Alignment with Beacon
Leadership has read the Beacon Replication Toolkit Overview and Strategic Plan
The organization is willing to submit monthly data reports to Beacon HQ
The organization agrees to the Beacon partner branding and quality standards
The organization commits to annual MOU renewal and partner network participation
