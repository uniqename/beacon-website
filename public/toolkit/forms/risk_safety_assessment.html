Part B — Risk Score & Level
Risk Level Guide:
LOW 0–3 / no high-risk — Standard safety planning. Review in 30 days.
MEDIUM 4–7 / no high-risk — Enhanced safety plan. Weekly case manager contact. Legal partner informed.
HIGH / CRITICAL 8+ or any ⚠ — Immediate safety escalation. Legal partner contact today. Consider police notification. Daily check-in.
