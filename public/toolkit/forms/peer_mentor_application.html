Module 05 — Counseling
Form: BNB-05c Application Date: Reviewed by:
Peer mentors are volunteer survivors who have completed the Beacon program, demonstrated stability for at least 6 months post-exit, and wish to support others. Peer mentors are not case managers — they offer lived experience, encouragement, and practical peer support only. All mentors receive a brief orientation before starting.
