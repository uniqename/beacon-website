Module 07 — Digital & App
Form: BNB-07a Case #: Date: Staff:
Do this assessment before giving the survivor access to any Beacon digital tools. If ANY monitoring concern is identified, follow the device safety protocol below before proceeding. Never access the survivor's devices without their explicit consent.
Section 1 — Device Check
QuestionYes / No / UnsureAction if Yes
Does the survivor currently have a phone or device with them?
Was this device given to them or purchased by the perpetrator?
Does the perpetrator know the device's phone number?
Has the perpetrator ever accessed the survivor's phone without permission?
Does the perpetrator know the device's password or PIN?
Is location sharing turned on (Google Maps, Find My, etc.)?
Does the survivor believe tracking or monitoring software may be installed?
Is the device connected to a shared account (Google/Apple Family, iCloud)?
Section 2 — Social Media & Accounts
QuestionYes / No / UnsureNotes / Action
Does the perpetrator follow the survivor on any social media?
Does the perpetrator know the survivor's email address / password?
Are there shared accounts (banking, utilities) the perpetrator can access?
Does the perpetrator have access to the survivor's WhatsApp or messaging apps?
