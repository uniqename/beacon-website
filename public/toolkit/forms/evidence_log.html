Module 04 — Legal Advocacy

Important: Never alter evidence. Advise survivors not to delete anything from their devices — even deleted messages may be recoverable. Contact your legal partner immediately if there is an upcoming court date. Share this log only with your legal partner, not externally.
How to use: Record each piece of evidence as a separate row. Evidence types include: photos of injuries, medical records, screenshots of threatening messages, voicemails, witness statements, police reports, broken objects, financial records showing control. Maintain a chain of custody — note who has the original, who has copies, and where they are stored.
