Month 1 — Foundation & Setup Dates: _____________ to _____________
Week 1 — Legal & Governance · Target date: _____________
File legal registration documents (NGO / 501c3). Engage a lawyer if needed.
Complete Site Readiness Assessment and submit with partner application to Beacon HQ.
→ Site Readiness
Submit Partner Application to Beacon HQ (ename@beaconnewbeginnings.org).
→ Partner App
Draft initial board structure — recruit 3–5 founding board members.
Open organizational bank account with dual-signatory controls.
Set up secure filing system for survivor documents (physical + digital).
✓
Milestone: Partner application submitted, legal filing in process.
Week 2 — Team & Partnerships · Target date: _____________
Post and begin hiring: Case Manager (required), Admin Coordinator (if budget allows).
→ Job Description
Contact 2–3 local legal aid organizations to discuss partnership.
→ Legal MOU
Contact 1–2 local healthcare/counseling providers to discuss partnership.
Sign Partner MOU with Beacon HQ (returned after application approval).
→ Partner MOU
Begin identifying shelter location — shortlist 2–3 options.
✓
Milestone: Case manager hired or shortlisted. At least 1 legal partner in discussion.
Week 3 — Shelter & Budget · Target date: _____________
Secure shelter location. Sign lease or access agreement.
Complete Shelter Setup Checklist for the physical space.
→ Shelter Checklist
Finalize Year 1 budget and submit to board for approval.
→ Budget Template
Set up grant tracking log — identify 5+ funders to apply to in months 1–3.
→ Grant Tracker
Purchase supplies: bedding, hygiene kits, lockable filing cabinet, first aid kit.
✓
Milestone: Shelter space secured and checklist complete. Budget approved by board.
Week 4 — Training & Protocols · Target date: _____________
Case manager and site lead complete Beacon trauma-informed care orientation (contact Beacon HQ to schedule).
Draft and finalize intake protocol — adapt forms to your local context.
→ Intake Form
Sign legal aid MOU with at least 1 legal partner.
→ Legal MOU
Run first local outreach event (church, community centre, hospital) to announce services.
Install Beacon app — configure local hotlines and language settings (contact Beacon HQ).
✓
Milestone: Team trained, legal MOU signed, intake protocol ready, app configured.

Submit first grant application from your tracker.
→ Grant Tracker

Milestone: First residents admitted and intake paperwork complete.
Week 6 — Case Management Begins · Target date: _____________
Complete Survivor Intake Assessment for each resident (48–72 hrs after arrival).
→ Assessment
Create 90-Day Individual Care Plan with each resident by end of Week 1 in shelter.
→ Care Plan
Make first referrals: legal, counseling, or healthcare as identified in care plan.
→ Legal Referral
Run first weekly house meeting with all residents.
Conduct first staff wellbeing check-in with case manager.
→ Staff Wellbeing

Milestone: All residents have care plans. First referrals made. Case files complete.

Submit Month 1 report to Beacon HQ via dashboard.

Complete first Progress Tracking form for each resident (30-day mark).
