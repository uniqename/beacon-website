Beacon of New Beginnings

Strategic Plan & Organizational Overview — 2026–2031
Enam Egyir Executive Director & Founder
ename@beaconnewbeginnings.org
Ghana · United States
"To provide safety, healing, and empowerment to survivors of abuse and homelessness through shelter, professional counseling, legal advocacy, and livelihood programs that restore dignity and independence — in Ghana and the United States."
The Problem
Domestic violence survivors in Ghana and the U.S. face fragmented support systems — shelter exists separately from counseling, legal help is disconnected from economic recovery, and safety tools are inaccessible in active danger. In Ghana, cultural barriers and limited DV infrastructure leave survivors with few formal options. Existing digital tools address one dimension; none provide holistic, cross-border, technology-enabled case management.
Our Approach
Beacon integrates four pillars into one ecosystem: shelter, counseling, legal advocacy, and livelihood empowerment. A trauma-informed mobile app — featuring disguised stealth mode, court-admissible evidence tools, safety planning, and job access — bridges digital support to physical shelter homes, allowing case managers to serve survivors from crisis through independence.
