RE: APPLICATION FOR LICENCE TO OPERATE AS A NON-PROFIT ORGANISATION

Dear Sir/Madam,

We, the undersigned, on behalf of the Board of Directors and Management of The BeaconPath Ghana, write to respectfully apply for a Licence to operate as a Non-Profit Organisation (NPO) under the Social Welfare regulations of Ghana.

The BeaconPath Ghana is a Non-Profit Organisation incorporated in Ghana under the Companies Act, 2019 (Act 992) as a company limited by guarantee. Our organisation is dedicated to providing comprehensive support, safety, and empowerment to survivors of domestic violence, abuse, and homelessness across Ghana.

We believe that every survivor deserves access to safety, healing, and the opportunity to rebuild their life with dignity. Our programmes span emergency shelter, trauma-informed counselling, legal advocacy, livelihood and vocational training, educational support for children of survivors, and a digital platform that connects individuals to crisis resources at any hour of the day.

We are committed to working in full collaboration with the Department of Social Welfare and all relevant government agencies to ensure that our operations remain compliant, accountable, and aligned with national social welfare priorities.

We enclose herewith all documents required for your review and consideration. We remain readily available at your earliest convenience for any further information, interview, or site visit that your office may require.

We trust that our application will receive your favourable consideration.

Yours faithfully,
