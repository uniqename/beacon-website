PILOT PHASE FRAMEWORK

BPG & The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd

90-Day Pilot Period | Start Date: _______________ | End Date: _______________

PURPOSE

This Pilot Phase Framework establishes the terms, structure, and success criteria for a 90-day trial partnership between The BeaconPath Ghana (BPG) and The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd. It responds directly to The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd's proposal to introduce a pilot phase to assess operational alignment, referral volume, and financial sustainability before committing to a full partnership agreement.

At the end of the pilot period, both parties will conduct a formal review to determine whether to proceed to a full partnership under the Memorandum of Understanding.

PILOT OBJECTIVES

  1. Test the referral pathway and intake process in real operational conditions
  2. Validate client category definitions and payment arrangements
  3. Assess communication and coordination between both organizations' liaison teams
  4. Identify any gaps, friction points, or improvements needed before full partnership launch
  5. Build confidence and trust between both organizations through shared experience

PILOT PARAMETERS

Duration

90 days from the date of MOU signing. Pilot start date and end date to be recorded above.

Referral Volume

During the pilot, BPG commits to referring a maximum of 10 clients to The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd. Both parties agree this cap allows for meaningful assessment without overwhelming either organization's capacity. The cap may be adjusted upward by mutual agreement if both teams are satisfied with early progress.

PAYMENT PROCESS DURING PILOT

  • BPG will confirm the client's payment category on the referral form at the time of submission
  • For partially supported clients, BPG will attach a written Payment Commitment Letter specifying the BPG contribution amount and payment timeline
  • For exceptional cases, BPG will submit a written Exceptional Case Request to The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd's liaison for prior approval before the referral is processed
  • No referral will proceed to service delivery until payment arrangements are confirmed in writing
  • The Brain and Sleep Medical Centre Ltd will invoice BPG directly for any agreed BPG-funded portions

PILOT REVIEW MEETING AGENDA

The 90-day review meeting will follow this structure:

  1. Referral volume and category breakdown — how many referrals were made, by category
  2. Process review — what worked, what didn't in the referral and payment pathway
  3. Financial sustainability check — were payment arrangements honored? Any disputes or delays?
  4. Client outcome check-in — any known outcomes or feedback from referred individuals
  5. Liaison relationship review — communication quality, response times, ease of coordination
  6. Decision: proceed to full MOU, modify terms, or extend pilot
