REFERRAL PATHWAY DOCUMENT

BNB & LEKMA Polyclinic

PURPOSE

This document defines the referral procedures between Beacon of New Beginnings (BNB) and LEKMA Polyclinic. It serves as Annex B to the Memorandum of Understanding between both organizations. LEKMA Polyclinic's Patient Support Service is the designated coordination hub for all referral activities under this partnership.

REFERRAL TYPES

1. BNB → LEKMA Polyclinic (via Patient Support Service)

BNB may refer clients to LEKMA Polyclinic when an individual requires primary medical care or health-related services and meets the following criteria:

  • The individual has been assessed by a BNB case manager and identified as needing medical support
  • The individual has provided informed, written consent to be referred
  • The referral is appropriate for LEKMA Polyclinic's level of care

2. LEKMA Polyclinic → BNB (via Patient Support Service)

LEKMA Polyclinic's Patient Support Service may refer patients to BNB when they present with one or more of the following:

  • Unstable housing or risk of homelessness
  • Need for vocational or life skills support
  • Legal issues requiring navigation assistance
  • Limited social support or risk of community isolation
  • Need for structured case management and follow-up after clinical care
  • Mental health support needs requiring counselling referral coordination

PATIENT SUPPORT SERVICE — SPECIFIC PROCEDURES

For referrals passing through LEKMA Polyclinic's Patient Support Service:

  • The Patient Support Service representative will receive all inbound BNB referral forms and log them immediately
  • The Patient Support Service will facilitate the patient's medical appointment and communicate any access or support needs to the relevant clinical team
  • For outbound referrals (LEKMA → BNB), the Patient Support Service will complete the referral form and submit directly to BNB's designated liaison
  • The Patient Support Service will maintain the physical or digital folder of BNB information materials for distribution to patients

REFERRAL FORM — MINIMUM REQUIRED INFORMATION

  • Client/patient full name and date of birth
  • Contact information (phone/address)
  • Reason for referral and presenting needs
  • Summary of current circumstances and any urgent concerns
  • Services currently being received (if any)
  • Name and contact of referring case manager or Patient Support Service representative
  • Signed client/patient consent form

TRACKING AND REVIEW

Both parties will maintain a shared referral log capturing:

  • Date of referral and direction
  • Client/patient identifier (anonymized as agreed)
  • Category and presenting need
  • Outcome of intake or consultation
  • 30-day and 90-day follow-up notes

The referral log will be reviewed at each partnership meeting (minimum bi-annual).

AMENDMENTS

This document may be updated by mutual written agreement of both parties. Any updates must be signed by the designated liaisons of both organizations.
